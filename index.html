Indonesia is known for its beautiful beaches. Places like Kuta Beach and Raja Ampat are examples of how beautiful Indonesia is. However, there are still lot of potentials that haven't been utilized for tourism in other area like in Sawarna Beach, Banten (picture 1), the problem lies on availiability and condition of the places to stay in that potential area. After discussing with a lot of expert, the fastest solution to utilize this potential is by working together with the villagers to make a good house out of the bamboo.

Why use Bamboo not wood or concrete ? First, Bamboo can be found anywhere especially in Banten and the price of Bamboo is cheaper than wood or concrete. Second, cutting bamboo has less enviromental impact because it only tooks five years to grow bamboo tree. Moreover, bamboo house can be build faster and with good design it looks nice and can attract a lot of tourist to come. The design in the picture can make the house floating in the water which can be helpful if the big waves come.
